HOW TO PLAY
  • 🖱️ Click the ground to walk. A glowing ring marks your destination.
  • 🌊 Wade into the shallows to swim across water — the deep sea is off-limits.
  • 🌹 Click flowers to harvest, 🌲 trees for wood, ⛏️ rocks for ore.
  • 🎣 Click the fishing ripples in the shallows to fish for food and scales.
  • ⚔️ Click a creature to fight it. Winning raises your Attack & Defense levels — watch the XP bars fill and level-up bursts flare. Wolves and bears strike first!
  • 🍖 Click food (meat, fish) in your inventory to eat and restore HP.
  • ⚗️ Craft in the Manufacture window — mixes can fail, so mind the odds shown on each recipe. Click finished gear to equip it.
  • 🔍 Mouse wheel zooms · hover items for instant tooltips.
  • ✨ Press N to open the Talent Tree — spend talent points (earned each ATK/DEF level) on Fire, Lightning, Ice, or Spirit skills.
  • 🎯 Skills hotbar at the bottom — assign skills from the Talent Tree, then press 1–5 to activate them.
